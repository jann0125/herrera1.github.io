ABOUT US

We started this business way back 2015 as a small bakery. Our customers are happy with what we have to offer - breads and cookies. The good feedbacks from them gave us the perfect sign to expand our business and added cakes and the likes to our menu. Just like other starting businesses, ours began with a lot of struggles especially losses. However, with determination to succeed and passion for pastries, we have overcome those rough roads that we took.

And now here we are, three years in the market. We already established 2 branches . Our products are not only affordable and but of good quality. We offer a lot of varieties starting from our classic cake down to our supreme cake. We even let our customers customize their cakes to satisfy their specific cravings.

For the sweet tooth, vegetarian, and even for those people who are on a diet but is celebrating cheat day, we let them control the sweetness and give them the option to choose if they will opt for a fruit/vegetable-based ingredients. This strategy differetiates us from the crowd. We value our customers by giving back their money's worth.

We are confident that despite the strong competition, we will remain stable and in line with the best bakeshops as we ensure that we always exceeds our customer's expectaions.
